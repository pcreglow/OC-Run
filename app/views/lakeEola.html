Lake Eola 2 Miler


Lake Eola offers a unique urban running environment. This 2 mile route provides outstanding scenery of Lake Eola and the downtown skyline.


Distance: 2 miles

  • Ammenities: Drinking fountain, bathrooms
  • Crowds: High during evening
  • Dog activity: Medium
  • Bike activity: Low
  • Surface: Wide pathways
